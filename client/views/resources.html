IN PURSUIT OF THE UNDISCOVERED

Learning

    The Hard Thing About Hard Things
  • Char Genevier
  • "Having moved from being a CTO at my last company to CEO at this company, which is a totally different thing for a venture-backed startup, which we are now, it’s like, it’s really just a whole new set of pressures. Where coding is super black and white, people problems, which is largely what CEO’s deal with, is so shades of grey. It’s nuanced. There are feelings involved, you can’t just like, solve the problem, you can’t solve the person. So, that’s been really hard for me, and the Hard Thing about Hard Things has really helped me think through the problems of being a CEO."

    Let My People Go Surfing
  • Jenna Walker
  • I think it’s really important when you start a business to know your core values and to know why you are doing what you’re doing. [This is] a book I buy for a lot of people in our company, because we can do it our way, right? Business doesn’t have to be one way or the other.

Technology

    Calm App
  • Char Genevier
  • "It’s an app for guided meditation, it’s not like you just set a timer. You pick a focus or sleep meditation and doing that everyday has completely changed my ability to deal with stress. I would recommend even five minutes a day, and if you stop doing it you start to notice, ‘oh my god I’m more irritable and I can’t deal with life.’"
